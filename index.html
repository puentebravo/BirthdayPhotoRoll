About This App

This app was created in honor of Florian "Floyd" Matos' 90th birthday. A veteran, adventurer, writer, and loving father, Floyd's life has taken him from New York to points all round the world. The photos contained here seek to explore, as much as they can, the incredible story that is his life.


Happy Birthday, Floyd. And to many more!
